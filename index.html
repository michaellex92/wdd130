Nigeria

Nigerian flag, designed by Michael Taiwo Akinkunmi, was officially adopted on October 1, 1960, symbolizing the country's independence and unity. Design and Symbolism. The flag consists of three vertical stripes: two green stripes on the sides and a white stripe in the center. The green represents Nigeria's lush agricultural landscape and natural resources, while the white symbolizes peace and unity among the diverse ethnic groups in the country.
